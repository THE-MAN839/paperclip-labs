## The Emerging Patterns

**1. World models are the next frontier.** Both Demis (Genie) and Fei-Fei (World Labs) are betting big here. After LLMs master language, they master 3D space.

**2. Agency > Intelligence.** Karpathy's insight - in a world where intelligence is "on tap" via AI, what matters is the drive to use it.

**3. Safe superintelligence as explicit goal.** Ilya's SSI is the first lab with a single product: safe superintelligence. No chatbots, no side projects.

**4. Open vs closed still matters.** LeCun keeps pushing open source. The debate isn't over.

**5. AI-native education.** Karpathy's Eureka Labs - a whole new kind of school built around AI.

**6. Spatial/3D AI heating up.** World Labs, Genie - turning static images into dynamic worlds.
